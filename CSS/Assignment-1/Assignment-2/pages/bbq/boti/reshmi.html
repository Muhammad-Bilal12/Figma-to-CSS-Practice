Reshmi Kabaab

Reshmi Kabab Recipe - Its a delicious Mughlai dish popular in Pakistan. It is prepared with minced meat in the form of patty and is grilled on the charcoal fire. This dish can be prepared with chicken, mutton, or beef minced meat. In order to make delicious Reshmi Kabab recipe, marinate the mince meat with spices and other ingredients for an hour. Reshmi Kabab recipe is usually prepared at special occasions like Eid barbecue party at home after making appropriate arrangements for charcoal fire. It is mostly served in restaurants in the barbeque section with Naan, Salad, or Green Chutney. Enjoy tender, juicy and delicious Reshmi Kabab recipe at home by following this method
